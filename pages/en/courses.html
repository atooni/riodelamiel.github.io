Current Courses

In addition to our normal daily schedule we regularly offer courses for beginners to find their way into a daily Yoga routine or for experienced Yoga practicioners to provide some inspiration for enriching their daily practice. Often we invite experienced Yoga teachers we have met ourselves or we have heard of, so that they can teach our guests and also ourselves with a fresh perspective.
