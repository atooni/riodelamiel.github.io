Reservations

  1. Reservations are accepted via the web site www.riodelamiel.eu, email or by phone. All reservations should specify the start and the end date of the stay and any courses the guest wants to participate in.

  2. All reservations will be answered by Rio de la Miel with either a confirmation or a denial in case the course is already sold out.

  3. In case a reservation is confirmed it will be guaranteed for 5 days. Within 5 days a deposit of 25% of the total price must be made to secure the booking. This should give enough time to arrange the flights for the visit.

  4. For Karma Yogis a confirmation from Rio de la Miel is sufficient for a permanent guarantee. We would ask Karmayogis to let us know as early as possible if they can’t come as planned.

  5. If the deposit is not made within 5 days, the reservation is forfeited and must be redone.

  6. The remaining balance is payable upon arrival.

Cancellations

  1. The stay at Rio de la Miel can be cancelled until 4 weeks before the stay. In that case the deposit is forfeited.

  2. If the stay is cancelled afterwards, the full amount of the stay is due. We recommendto consider a travel insurance to cover cases of illness etc.

  3. In case a course has to be cancelled due to lack of interest, the guest can choose between a cancellation free of charge and Yoga vacations at Rio de la Miel. In any of these cases any overpaid amount will be transferred to the guests bank account or the credit card used for payments.
